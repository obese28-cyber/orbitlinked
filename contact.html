About OrbitLinked

OrbitLinked Technology is a Ghana-based infrastructure company focused on satellite communication systems and digital connectivity deployment. We operate at the intersection of satellite broadband, enterprise networking, and regional telecom expansion.

Our mission is to bridge the connectivity gap across underserved regions through scalable, reliable, and cost-efficient satellite-enabled infrastructure.
